# Crystal Sanctum

> **Status:** design (2026-07-20)

## Context

The user seed is the **Crystal Sanctum**, the Spirit Conclave's main seat of power and architectural masterpiece. A Soul Crystal enables an ultra-strong, almost weightless construction system, so the landmark should express a civilization that treats gravity as a negotiable condition rather than the source of its architectural order. The discarded working title was “Spire of Eternia”; the established building name remains Crystal Sanctum.

This run follows [[concept-to-studio-architectural-build]]. C2H — Double Helix / As Above, So Below is selected and section decomposition is active. No asset owner or target place has been chosen, and no Studio scene is authorized for mutation.

### Continuity synthesis

The existing Soul Steel lore remains read-only and is not changed by this pipeline.

- **Known canon:** Crystal Sanctum is the Spirit Conclave's institutional headquarters, built above the Maw Between; the oldest Floating Isle is linked to a Soul Core fragment and is also described as a seat of Conclave power.
- **Known canon:** a genuine Soul Crystal is conscious, extraordinarily rare, found rather than manufactured, and cannot responsibly be treated as bulk aggregate.
- **Known canon:** Spirit practice is based on attunement, redirection, barriers, purification, vibration, and visible soul currents. The Conclave is both a real place of healing and a system that hides extraction beneath kindness.
- **Approved reconciliation:** Crystal Sanctum is one vertically distributed complex. Its visible ceremonial and governmental crown is suspended on the oldest Floating Isle directly above its mountain-bound institutional body. A veiled resonance route joins the two.
- **Proposed material rule:** one living Soul Crystal creates a tuned field in which an ordinary soul-resonant crystal lattice becomes ultra-strong and nearly weightless. The building is not made from pulverized Soul Crystal. Public doctrine calls the relationship a covenant; whether the crystal's consent is genuine remains deliberately unresolved.

## Refined design contract

### Thesis

**A cathedral held together by agreement.** Crystal Sanctum is not a stack of floors. It is a family of inhabited masses held in phase around an uninterrupted column of light. Voids replace conventional load-bearing contact. From far away it reads as one unmistakable vertical glyph; from within, it feels like moving through a chord whose notes are rooms, bridges, and suspended courts.

The working parti is a three-part vertical composition:

1. **The Ascendant Spire** — the luminous public landmark: worship, healing, diplomacy, ceremony, and the Conclave's claim that the Epoch of Eternity can endure.
2. **The Isle Hinge** — the oldest Floating Isle as civic ground: arrival, gardens, guest cloisters, security thresholds, and the point where ordinary orientation briefly returns.
3. **The Veiled Root** — a smaller inverted spire below the island: administration, restricted archives, field-control systems, and the silent connection toward Crystal Sanctum. Its existence is obvious only from forbidden viewpoints.

### First-principles kernel

- **Boundary:** every major chamber is a discrete floating volume, separated by a readable band of air or light. Crossing a boundary is an event, never an unnoticed doorway.
- **State:** architecture changes with attunement. Bridges clarify, rooms rotate into alignment, membranes become transparent, and sound shifts from choir-like harmony to near-silence.
- **Relationship:** the central light-axis, not gravity, is the ordering reference. Distance, orientation, access, and status are expressed by how a volume relates to that axis.
- **Transition:** public travel ascends in a slow outward helix; service and forbidden travel descends inward through short, controlled crossings. The routes occasionally see one another but do not meet.
- **Order:** concentric resonance bands set hierarchy: open civic functions at the outer ring, sacred and governmental functions nearer the axis, and concealed control functions inside or below it.

### Structural and visual laws

- No primary mass rests conventionally on the mass below it; deliberate gaps are part of the silhouette.
- The central axis remains visible or acoustically perceptible from every important public room.
- Near-weightless does not mean arbitrary: every floating piece must visibly register an attachment, orbit, alignment, or resonance relationship.
- Structural failure reads first as dissonance, drift, rotation, or loss of transparency—not ordinary cracking and collapse.
- Repetition uses a small modular kit of ribs, crystal membranes, ring segments, bridge anchors, and inhabited pods. Exceptional forms are reserved for the heart, council chamber, and principal threshold.
- Ornament is cyber-gothic and systemic: luminous seams, tuned ribs, floating tracery, conductor-like anchors, and inscriptions encoded as vibration rather than pasted decoration.

Connector families are part of the architecture rather than generic hallways:

- **Processional helix:** broad, slow, accessible ascent with long views and gathering landings.
- **Resonance bridges:** thin straight or arcing crossings that become visually solid only when attuned.
- **Veil lifts:** near-weightless vertical chambers moving along the central field without visible rails.
- **Quiet passages:** short enclosed service links inside ribs and ring thicknesses; low ceilings and muted acoustics contrast with the public volume.

### Scale and sensory target

- **Provisional landmark envelope:** approximately 1,200-1,600 studs from highest fin to lowest root, with a 420-520 stud civic crown. Only the core traversal volume must be fully playable; distant fins and repeated tracery may be simplified shells.
- **Player-scale rhythm:** 8-12 stud intimate passages, 24-40 stud chambers, 60-120 stud civic rooms, and a 180+ stud ceremonial void. A Roblox avatar must remain readable at every transition.
- **Sight:** opaline white and pale spirit-glass against deep blue-violet shadow; cold cyan soul currents; restrained gold trim becomes more common along administrative and forbidden routes.
- **Sound:** a low even hum resolves into chords near public gathering rooms, then loses overtones one by one during descent until only the field's pulse remains.
- **Kinesthetic:** gentle current-like lift near the axis, almost imperceptible rotation across bridges, and a return of bodily weight inside the concealed administrative core.

### Three concept-image directions

All three directions preserve the site, program, living-crystal field, dual circulation, palette, and upward/hidden-downward moral structure. They differ in governing geometry and spatial experience, not merely surface treatment.

1. **A — The Eternal Chord (axial):** one extremely slender double-ended spine, with inhabited rings and council volumes orbiting it like notes on a staff. Strongest distant emblem and clearest Ascendant Spire / Veiled Root duality.
2. **B — The Weightless Cloister (branching):** a central heart opens into asymmetric cathedral petals, hanging gardens, and rib-like bridges. Most humane and monastic; gravity-defiance reads through cantilevered inhabited wings and open-air cloisters.
3. **C — The Crowned Void (orbital):** nested habitable halos surround a colossal empty core, with the actual spire implied by aligned absence. Most alien and contemplative; strongest top-down plan and threshold drama.

### C2H — Double Helix / As Above, So Below

The operator selected C2 as the refinement base, requested a double-helix silhouette governed by “as above, so below,” and locked the resulting C2H direction on 2026-07-20. The selected reference is `output/imagegen/crystal-sanctum/crystal-sanctum-concept-c2h-double-helix-as-above-so-below.png` (1672 × 941; SHA-256 `7ff334115dcb3dabe81b73b25bed58d622fbb8282713c0062f666a16cdea9595`).

The architectural rule is now explicit:

- The Floating Isle and principal threshold form the equatorial plane.
- Two distinct, phase-offset inhabited strands spiral upward around the open Soul Crystal axis.
- Short bridge-chambers join the strands at controlled intervals without filling the central void.
- An equal inverted pair descends below: the same height, pitch, floor capacity, window rhythm, and connector order.
- The upper helix is luminous, ceremonial, and publicly legible; the lower helix is equally substantial but colder, administratively coded, and access-controlled.

This selected direction gives the clearest structural expression of the Conclave's duality and a repeatable Roblox kit: curved multi-floor strand segments, equatorial threshold modules, bridge-chamber rungs, axial crystal anchors, and mirrored upper/lower zone assemblies. The image remains speculative support art rather than a production asset or lore-graph capture.

### Decisions for the refinement gate

The operator approved the integrated canon reading and progression into A/B/C visual development. The current foundation is:

1. Treat the **Ascendant Spire as the upper crown of Crystal Sanctum**, with the Sanctum name applying to the whole distributed complex.
2. Make the **Veiled Root physically real but publicly hidden**, so the architecture carries the Conclave's dual nature without turning the public landmark into an obvious villain fortress.
3. Keep the Soul Crystal relationship **beautiful but ethically ambiguous**: its field is cooperative in doctrine, while its restraints can also be read as either life support or captivity.

The candidate creative-bundle path is `Assets/models/architectural-environments/crystal-sanctum/`, but it remains uncreated until an asset owner and category are explicitly selected.

Until that owner gate closes, runtime-neutral draft section records live at `output/architecture/crystal-sanctum/sections/`; they may be migrated into the canonical bundle without changing their stable `section_id` values.

The initial decomposition contains 14 validated records spanning site, equatorial mass, arrival threshold, mirrored helix systems, reusable strand and bridge modules, Soul Crystal axis, five principal occupied programs, and the concealed administration/root sequence. The bundle contract and coordinate system are recorded in `output/architecture/crystal-sanctum/README.md`. Three foundational conceptual sheets cover the mirrored master section, equatorial plan, and strand/bridge connection family. Two room-scale sheets add the Choir of Confluence and the north Diplomacy Court; both room-scale designs received operator approval on 2026-07-20. The remaining requested views are still open inside this phase.

## Build plan

1. **Seed** (done 2026-07-20): record the user fantasy, power source, faction role, and gravity-light material premise.
2. **Refine** (done 2026-07-20): approve the continuity reconciliation, ethical ambiguity of the living crystal, shared architectural laws, and A/B/C comparison axes.
3. **Concept images** (done 2026-07-20): generated and visually inspected one matched presentation image for each direction; stable paths and hashes recorded above.
4. **Selection** (done 2026-07-20): C2H — Double Helix / As Above, So Below locked as the governing direction.
5. **Sections and diagrams** (active): 14 stable section records created and cross-validated on 2026-07-20. Five sheets are now generated, visually inspected, hashed, and linked: three foundational system views plus the operator-approved Choir of Confluence room blueprint and Diplomacy Court subspace blueprint. Generate only the remaining plan, section, elevation, axonometric, circulation, or connection views each section requests.
6. **Manifest**: compile parts, materials, assemblies, effects, assets, budgets, tags, connectors, and build order into `roblox-build-manifest.json`.
7. **Owner and Studio gates**: select the asset owner, TGL domain, target place, and reversible Edit-mode staging root before any MCP mutation.
8. **Build and verify**: construct from the manifest, audit traversal and performance, capture evidence, persist through the approved snapshot/syncback path, and leave promotion unauthorized by default.

## Exit criteria

- The operator approves the shared concept contract and one of the three visual comparison axes.
- Three structurally distinct concept images are reviewed and one direction or explicit synthesis is selected.
- Every selected section has stable metadata, required diagrams, dimensions, adjacency, material tokens, Roblox translation, and acceptance checks.
- The build manifest distinguishes primitives, registered assets, local-only assets, generated candidates, and placeholders, with explicit budgets and fallbacks.
- The intended owner and Edit-mode Studio target are confirmed before mutation; the resulting blockout is reversible, playable, audited, and persisted with evidence.
- No concept art, local `BLK` asset, Studio blockout, or generated lore is represented as production canon or promotion without a separate owner decision.
