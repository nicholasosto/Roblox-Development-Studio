# Equatorial Oculus

The oculus is the project datum. Its clear opening is provisionally 220 studs in diameter inside a 720-stud overall civic ring.

## Boundary / State / Relationship / Transition / Order

- **Boundary:** a luminous inner guard membrane separates occupied civic ground from the open axial void without hiding it.
- **State:** the public surface is bright and open; restricted apertures below clarify only for authorized attunements.
- **Relationship:** every strand begins or terminates tangentially at the annulus, while the Soul Crystal remains untouched at center.
- **Transition:** arrival moves radially inward; helix circulation departs tangentially upward or downward.
- **Order:** cardinal courts organize arrival, diplomacy, service, and contemplation around one shared center.

## Player path and sightlines

The player can orbit the complete oculus, see across it, and understand which helix strand rises or descends from each quadrant. Guarding must not rely on invisible walls near the void.

## Diplomacy Court subspace

The north cardinal court uses a provisional 176-stud tangential width, 128-stud radial depth, and 48-stud occupied height. Equal east and west delegation approaches enter two interleaved seating arcs around a neutral accord floor. The public witness gallery remains outside the delegate arcs, while a quiet service ring runs behind it without crossing either diplomatic route. The inner spirit-glass guard preserves the direct sightline to the Soul Crystal axis; no position is treated as a throne or privileged head of table.

## Roblox translation

Build the annulus from repeated 15-degree wedge or mesh-candidate segments, with a simpler blockout ring first. Separate floor, inner guard, outer facade, and underside assemblies so collision and LOD can be tuned independently.
