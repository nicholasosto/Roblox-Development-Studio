# Veiled Double Helix

The lower system is not a decorative root. It matches the upper helix in height, pitch, clear width, connector rhythm, and usable capacity.

## Boundary / State / Relationship / Transition / Order

- **Boundary:** colder membranes, narrower apertures, and gold-coded thresholds distinguish access without changing the mirrored geometry.
- **State:** spaces lose ambient overtones and transparency as they descend; the axial pulse becomes more physically perceptible.
- **Relationship:** every major lower datum has an upper counterpart, but paired functions expose the institution's public and hidden readings.
- **Transition:** descent is controlled by short crossings and permissions rather than a single guarded door.
- **Order:** archives and ordinary administration occupy the upper descent; field control and restricted transfer occupy the deepest turns.

## Player path and sightlines

The lower route must feel equally extensive yet harder to survey. Players occasionally glimpse the bright upper mirror through the oculus, reinforcing orientation and moral contrast.

## Roblox translation

Reuse the same geometric strand kit as the Ascendant half. Differentiate through material tokens, lighting, transparency, room inserts, doors, and audio rather than a second structural asset family.
