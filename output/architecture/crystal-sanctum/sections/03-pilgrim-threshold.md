# Pilgrim Threshold

## Boundary / State / Relationship / Transition / Order

- **Boundary:** a low, shaded arrival portico creates the last ordinary room before the impossible axial volume.
- **State:** exterior wind and cloud noise diminish; the Sanctum's harmonic field becomes audible as the inner membrane clears.
- **Relationship:** the entry axis is deliberately offset from the Soul Crystal so the full void is discovered rather than approached head-on.
- **Transition:** compression, cleansing pause, lateral turn, then full-height reveal.
- **Order:** visitors enter through the broad center; staff and security occupy shallow flanking bays that read as hospitality desks.

## Player path and sightlines

The threshold must provide a memorable reveal without trapping the camera or creating a blinding backlight. From the release point, the player sees an upper strand, its mirrored lower counterpart, and at least one bridge-chamber.

## Roblox translation

Use a simple modular portico shell, translucent inner membrane, restrained particles, and a scripted lighting/audio state only after the static blockout proves the reveal.

## Acceptance checks

- [ ] A 6-stud avatar remains readable against the release view.
- [ ] The full reveal occurs after a clear turn or aperture, not immediately on bridge spawn.
- [ ] Accessibility route and ceremonial stair reach the same datum.
- [ ] Security bays do not constrict the primary group flow.
