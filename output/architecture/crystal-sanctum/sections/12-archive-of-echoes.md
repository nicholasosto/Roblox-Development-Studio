# Archive of Echoes

The archive is the intentional seam between the two moral readings of the Sanctum. Its public reading rooms sit above the equator; its colder record vaults continue below using the same spatial grammar.

## Boundary / State / Relationship / Transition / Order

- **Boundary:** concentric crystal stacks are navigable by harmonic channels; access boundaries appear as changes in resonance rather than barred cages.
- **State:** selected records clarify and sound; restricted bands remain visually present but acoustically mute.
- **Relationship:** a vertical reading well links public scholarship to hidden ledgers without granting direct access.
- **Transition:** descent gradually replaces open study terraces with narrow controlled listening cells.
- **Order:** public index, scholar stacks, supervised records, restricted ledgers, sealed residue archive.

## Player path and sightlines

Players can always locate the return route by the equatorial light datum. Restricted spaces are foreshadowed through visible depth and fading sound rather than hidden behind indistinguishable walls.

## Roblox translation

Use repeated crystal-stack modules, simple listening consoles, clear access gates, and a central vertical well. Encoded vibration is represented first through lighting and material state; interactive retrieval is outside the architectural slice.
