# Ascendant Double Helix

Each strand completes approximately 1.5 turns around a 190-stud centerline radius. The pair is phase-offset 180 degrees and never fuses into a single ramp.

## Boundary / State / Relationship / Transition / Order

- **Boundary:** continuous exterior ribs and interior courtyards distinguish each strand while open air remains visible between them.
- **State:** public segments are transparent and bright; governmental segments become more opaque nearer the crown.
- **Relationship:** both strands share pitch and radius, using bridge-chambers as periodic social and structural relationships.
- **Transition:** ascent alternates long inhabited arcs with short bridge decisions, preventing an endless-ramp experience.
- **Order:** ceremony begins near the equator, healing occupies the middle rise, governance and archives occupy the upper turns.

## Player path and sightlines

At any point, players should see the other strand, the central axis, or the equatorial ring. Resting rooms interrupt the slope; no continuous walk exceeds roughly 180 studs without a destination or choice.

## Roblox translation

Assemble from repeated `helix-strand-module` units rotated around the axis with a constant rise. Treat exterior facade, walkable floors, ribs, windows, and room inserts as separate collections. Exact curve meshes remain candidates until primitive blockout proves pitch and clearance.
