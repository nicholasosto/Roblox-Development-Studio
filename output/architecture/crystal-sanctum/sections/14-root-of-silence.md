# Root of Silence

This record ends the currently buildable Sanctum. The onward resonance route is a controlled threshold, not yet a designed tunnel or Maw chamber.

## Boundary / State / Relationship / Transition / Order

- **Boundary:** the two lower strands converge around a sealed transfer aperture while remaining separated from the Soul Crystal exclusion field.
- **State:** harmonic overtones have vanished; only the slow axial pulse and warm administrative vibration remain.
- **Relationship:** the room is the exact moral counterweight to the upper crown: equally monumental, almost entirely unseen.
- **Transition:** descent slows into a level inspection court, then terminates at a sealed resonant gate.
- **Order:** arrival arcs, inspection perimeter, control dais, sealed aperture, axial exclusion edge.

## Player path and sightlines

The player can see that infrastructure continues without seeing or entering the unresolved destination. The return helix remains legible; the gate dominates through silence and scale rather than overt menace.

## Roblox translation

Build a static sealed threshold with mirrored strand end caps, inspection floor, conductor cage, and inaccessible continuation marker. Do not construct the Maw, a tithe route, or a mountain transfer system until separately scoped.
