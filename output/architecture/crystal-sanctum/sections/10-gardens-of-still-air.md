# Gardens of Still Air

## Boundary / State / Relationship / Transition / Order

- **Boundary:** low planting beds, shallow water, and a transparent outer membrane form a room without blocking the cloud horizon.
- **State:** local air currents settle, sound softens, and subtle field lift reduces bodily weight.
- **Relationship:** the garden connects the two strands socially through a nearby bridge-chamber while remaining a pause rather than a shortcut.
- **Transition:** hard crystalline circulation becomes warmer, softer, and more tactile across a short planted threshold.
- **Order:** active path at the inner edge, social planting courts at center, quiet healing bays at the outer horizon.

## Player path and sightlines

The primary path stays obvious through planting. Seated or healing players occupy alcoves outside the circulation line; the opposite helix remains visible through selected openings.

## Roblox translation

Prefer built-in materials and low-complexity plant assets until registry readiness is known. Use repeated planter modules, shallow non-swimmable water planes, and bounded atmospheric effects.
