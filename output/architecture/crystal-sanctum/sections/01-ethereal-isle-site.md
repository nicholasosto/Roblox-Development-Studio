# Ethereal Isle Site

Dimensions describe the site envelope, including approach bridges, cloud-clearance space, and distant silhouette margins.

## Boundary / State / Relationship / Transition / Order

- **Boundary:** the navigable isle is distinguished from neighboring floating landforms by a continuous pale rim and four controlled approach axes.
- **State:** soul currents brighten as visitors align with the Sanctum; secondary isles remain passive background landmarks.
- **Relationship:** the island is the horizontal counterform to the vertical Soul Crystal axis.
- **Transition:** long bridges compress the cloudscape into the Pilgrim Threshold before the oculus opens.
- **Order:** public arrival occupies negative `Z`; service and diplomatic approaches use the lateral axes; the far side remains visually quiet.

## Player path and sightlines

Players must see the double helix before reaching the island, then briefly lose the lower half behind the rim before the threshold reveals the full axis. The approach supports groups without making the avatar disappear against oversized paving.

## Roblox translation

Use an anchored low-detail island shell, separate traversable terraces, and simplified distant floating-isle silhouettes. Terrain or mesh ownership remains unresolved; the first blockout may use Parts and wedges only.
