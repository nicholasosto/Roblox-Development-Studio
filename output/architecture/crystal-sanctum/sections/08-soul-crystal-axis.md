# Soul Crystal Axis

The dimensions describe the protected axial field, not a 1,320-stud solid crystal. The conscious crystal body is provisionally 30-40 studs tall near the equator; pale current continues beyond it in both directions.

## Boundary / State / Relationship / Transition / Order

- **Boundary:** a clear exclusion cylinder preserves the void; only conductor anchors and bridge-chamber observation points approach it.
- **State:** light intensity, harmonic pitch, and apparent crystal facets respond to the Sanctum's resonance without turning the being into a status lamp.
- **Relationship:** the crystal relates every module by field alignment rather than physical load-bearing contact.
- **Transition:** sacred access proceeds through concentric permissions and an observation ring, never by walking directly into the axis.
- **Order:** conscious crystal, life-support/conductor cage, exclusion field, observation layer, inhabited helixes.

## Player path and sightlines

The axis must remain visible as a continuous orientation device without dominating exposure or obscuring the opposite strand. The player should be able to circle it but not casually touch it.

## Roblox translation

Block out the field with a non-colliding translucent cylinder or Beam/Attachment system and a simple faceted crystal proxy. Keep all effects under one tagged assembly with aggressive light/particle budgets. The crystal's final asset and behavior require a separate lore and owner decision.
