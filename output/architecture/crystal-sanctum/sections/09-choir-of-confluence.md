# Choir of Confluence

## Boundary / State / Relationship / Transition / Order

- **Boundary:** an expanded strand bay opens toward the axis while acoustic ribs enclose the outer wall.
- **State:** ordinary speech remains intimate; ceremonial resonance distributes one voice across the room without visible amplification hardware.
- **Relationship:** tiered seating faces both a speaking platform and the Soul Crystal axis, making doctrine and power share one backdrop.
- **Transition:** the helix circulation widens gradually into the room rather than entering through a conventional door.
- **Order:** procession edge, gathering floor, tiered audience, ritual platform, axial aperture.

## Player path and sightlines

Players arriving from either direction see the gathering focus without crossing the active platform. Multiple exits prevent ceremonial crowds from reversing through one narrow helix segment.

## Roblox translation

Use stepped Part seating, repeated acoustic ribs, a clear-span floor, and a large spirit-glass axial membrane. Audio behavior remains out of scope until the room proves navigation and camera clearance.
