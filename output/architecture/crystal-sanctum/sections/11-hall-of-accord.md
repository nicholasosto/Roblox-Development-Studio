# Hall of Accord

## Boundary / State / Relationship / Transition / Order

- **Boundary:** two opposing strand volumes meet through a broadened bridge-chamber, making the council literally dependent on both halves.
- **State:** public sessions open the outer membranes; closed deliberation increases opacity without eliminating the axial silhouette.
- **Relationship:** seats form two interleaved arcs rather than a throne line, while Entabular's position aligns most precisely with the axis.
- **Transition:** delegates cross a neutral vestibule before entering the shared chamber from their assigned strand.
- **Order:** observer edge, delegate arcs, speaking floor, axial alignment point, concealed staff/service perimeter.

## Player path and sightlines

The player's entry should expose the full seating geometry and central alignment without forcing a walk across the speaking floor. Observer routes and diplomatic routes remain distinct.

## Roblox translation

Compose from two enlarged strand modules plus a custom bridge floor, repeated seat blocks, facade membranes, and concealed service galleries. The exceptional ceiling and council furniture remain placeholder primitives until selection of an asset owner.

- [ ] Both helix strands visibly contribute to the room geometry.
